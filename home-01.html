Bringing blockchains to the people.

IOV is a simple, secure and reliable system to help you send, receive or create your own digital assets from your preferred device.

IOV solves the problem of complexity, human error, lack of interoperability when exchanging digital assets on the blockchain.

IOV Name Service.
The first DNS for blockchains.

IOV build the first decentralized DNS for Blockchains. It is the backbone of the internet of values. IOV Name Service lists all active blockchains, their respective tokens and the IP addresses of their bootstrap nodes.

A Universal Value Specification. For Blockchain.

By implementing the Univeral Value Specification, IOV Wallet will be the first wallet to be able to send, receive and exchange any kind of cryptocurrency from a single address of value.

Right now, for each blockchain, the end-user needs to have a different wallet. IOV will provide the first revolutionary wallet which can access all tokens of a user.

A human address to avoid human error.

In order to solve the problem of the human error, such as sending a bitcoin to a wrong address, it would be allowed one single address for all coins for the end-user.

  • Instead of having an address like: 05ce0cf717c37a97c057fcf16d354800a85
  • IOV provides one like: john*iov

An atomic cross standard to make blockchains interoperable.

Thanks to the Univeral Value Specification and the IOV deployment tools, Blockchain creators will have the ability to make their blockchain interoperable and to exchange Tokens without the need to go into an exchange.

The IOV Token. The fuel of the IOV Name Service

The IOV coin is the staking token of the IOV Blockchain. IOV coin is also needed to interact with the IOV Blockchain. To register or update the token definition of a blockchain, the user needs to send a transaction on the IOV Blockchain with fees paid in IOV coin.
